Navotas Day

Hugal, Robert Jr. I.
Habig, Alexander

Navotas City was established on December 20, 1827, however Navotas celebrates its foundation day every January 16. Every foundation day, Navotas thinks of some new ideas to celebrate the day. The Navotas Day Celebration Committees lines up week-long festivities more often to bring joy to its citizens and hosts different events and variety shows with different artists and bands to perform. Parade of different groups and sector of Navotas. The festival also hosts dance contests and competition showdowns to promote the culture and tradition of Navotas, another event highlights were "Araw ng Mangingisda" where the city government will recognize top ten fisherfolks for their good standing in the community, Navotas Day also features the beauties of Navotas where they showcase their beauties and talents, and promote the advocacies of the city, the Navotas Motor Shows where they feature vintage and contemporary cars and motorbikes, the Kalye Fiesta food park that promotes locally-made products and home-grown delicacies, the Aero Zumba Competition for Navotenos of all ages, and the Pamilyang Navoteno Family Day, a fun-filled day for kids and the whole family, also were the Boodle Fight, Misa ng Pasasalamat, and Pistang Kristiyano.

Varieties of Programs during Navotas Day



Grand Parade


Every Navotas Day, there were lists of representatives of every barangays and different celebrities that will be shown for the parade. The streets were closed during the parade and many people across the streets were waiting for the parade to come by.


Mutya ng Navotas

Mutya ng Navotas is a beauty competition that was held every Navotas Day. Beautiful women from Navotas compete with each other, to represent their streets, with the aim to win a grand prize. It is like other beauty competitions with the women were having talent portions, q & a, long gowns, and many more.

Fireworks Display

Fireworks are being displayed at the beautiful sky of Navotas every 12 midnight exactly at the time of Navotas Day. It is displayed to be shown to the people of Navotas to celebrate the Day.

Bands and Artists

Many different bands and artists perform to hundreds of Navotenos to entertain them while waiting for the firework displays. They were also invited for the Navotenos to meet different artists and listen to beautiful songs from the bands.

Pangisdaan Competition

Pangisdaan Competition is held during the Navotas Day with the aim to make the Navotenos have fun and showcase the Navotas being the “Fishing Capital of the Philippines”. Different schools have its representatives in competing who is the best group dancers out of all with the concept of Fishing.
