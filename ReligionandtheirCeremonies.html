Church Of Christ (Iglesia Ni Cristo)

The Church of Christ (Iglesia Ni Cristo) worship services are performed by singing of hymns, prayers, sermon, and collection of voluntary contributions. INC holds two worship services per week: one midweek service (Wednesday or Thursday), and a Sunday service. Both men and women wear formal attire during services. ....

Hugal, Robert Jr. I.
Habig, Alexander
